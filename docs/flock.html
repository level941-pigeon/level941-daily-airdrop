how attribution works

for each new qualifying wallet, we find who paid for its very first transaction (the fee payer of a fresh wallet's oldest signature — a wallet with 0 SOL can't submit its own first transaction). that funder only gets credit if: they were already a qualifying holder before funding the new wallet; the new wallet's tokens did not come directly from the funder (splitting your own bag is not recruiting); and the new wallet sustains qualification for 3 days, not a snapshot-and-dump. funders with an unusual cluster of same-day recruits are flagged "under review" rather than hidden — this board exists to be watched before anything is ever paid out.
